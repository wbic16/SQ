  • Q: What's a small file transfer? (10 ms transit)
  • 10 ms @ 56 Kbps => 70 bytes (2000)
  • 10 ms @ 10 Mbps => 12.5 KB (2005)
  • 10 ms @ 1 Gbps = 1250 KB (2015)
  • TL;DR: With a modern gigabit fiber connection, you can download one wikipedia article per millisecond.
